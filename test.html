Shwezigon Pagoda

Shwezigon Pagoda is one of the most significant religious structures in Bagan. It is believed to enshrine a bone and tooth of Gautama Buddha. With its golden stupa and detailed architecture, it stands as a remarkable example of early Burmese temple design.
